Speech-Language Pathology

Speech Sound Disorders (Misarticulation)

We help children and adults who have difficulty producing specific sounds or sound patterns, making their speech hard to understand.

Causes & Symptoms: Often developmental, sometimes related to hearing or physical structure. Symptoms include substituting ('wabbit' for 'rabbit'), omitting, or distorting sounds.

Diagnosis: Comprehensive assessment including articulation tests and oral mechanism examination.

Therapy: Focuses on teaching correct sound production, phonological awareness, and generalizing new skills into everyday conversation. **Targeted, play-based approach.**

FAQ: When should I seek help? - If speech is difficult to understand by age 4, or if the child is frustrated by miscommunication.

Stuttering (Fluency Disorder)

We provide compassionate, evidence-based therapy to help clients achieve more fluent communication and manage the emotional impact of stuttering.

Causes & Symptoms: Neurodevelopmental origin. Symptoms include repetitions, prolongations, and blocks, often accompanied by secondary behaviors (e.g., eye blinking).

Therapy: For children, we use indirect therapy and programs like the **Lidcombe Program**. For adults, we focus on **Fluency Shaping** and **Stuttering Modification** techniques.

FAQ: Does stuttering go away? - Many children recover naturally, but intervention is crucial to prevent the disorder from becoming chronic.

Voice Disorders

Treatment for issues related to pitch, loudness, or quality, including hoarseness, nodules, and functional voice problems.

Diagnosis: Requires a medical clearance from an ENT specialist first. Assessment focuses on vocal function and hygiene.

Therapy: Techniques like **Vocal Function Exercises** and counseling on **Vocal Hygiene** to eliminate misuse/abuse and restore healthy voice production.

Autism & Developmental Communication Delay

Specialized intervention focused on improving social communication, language comprehension, joint attention, and functional speech skills.

Therapy: We use approaches like **ABA (Applied Behavior Analysis) principles, PECS (Picture Exchange Communication System)**, and naturalistic teaching methods.

Focus: Enhancing functional communication, play skills, and social interaction.

Audiology & Hearing Care

Diagnostic Audiometry Tests

Accurate and reliable testing for all ages to determine the presence, type, and severity of hearing loss.

  • **Pure Tone Audiometry (PTA):** Standard test to determine hearing thresholds.
  • **Otoacoustic Emissions (OAE):** Newborn and difficult-to-test population screening.
  • **Brainstem Evoked Response Audiometry (BERA/ASSR):** Objective measurement of hearing loss for infants and non-cooperative clients.
  • **Tympanometry:** Measures middle ear function and pressure.

FAQ: Do I need a referral? - No, you can book an appointment directly for diagnostic testing.

Hearing Aid Selection & Fitting

We offer the latest digital hearing technology, personalized to your hearing loss and lifestyle needs.

Process: Consultation, detailed testing, recommendation of appropriate style (BTE, RIC, CIC), precise digital programming, follow-up, and maintenance.

Technology: Specializing in all leading brands, including rechargeable, invisible, and smart technology hearing aids.
