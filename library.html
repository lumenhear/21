Aphasia

Speech & Language

A communication disorder that results from damage to the parts of the brain that contain language, causing difficulties with speaking, reading, writing, and understanding.

Target Audience: Adults, Geriatric

Delayed Speech

Pediatric

Occurs when a child misses the normal developmental milestones for speech and language compared to their peers.

Target Audience: Parents, Children
